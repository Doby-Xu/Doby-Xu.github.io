01 About

I am a Master's student in FVL Lab at the School of Computer Science, Fudan University, advised by Xingjun Ma and Yu-gang Jiang. I received my B.S. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am currently a research intern at Tencent Hunyuan, working on improving unified model consistency across a large number of reference subjects.

My research spans generative AI, mathematical properties of Transformers, privacy-preserving ML, and model IP protection. I am open to PhD opportunities and research collaborations.
